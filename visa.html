How to Apply for Canada Visitor Visa

The Canada Visitor Visa is for people who want to visit Canada for tourism, business, or family reasons. Here's how to apply:

  • Step 1: Gather required documents (proof of financial support, passport, etc.)
  • Step 2: Complete the visitor visa application form online.
  • Step 3: Pay the visa fee and submit your application.
  • Step 4: Wait for approval and your visa.
